WELCOME TO WP-CPROTEXT

Thanks for installing the CPROTEXT.COM online service WordPress plugin.

The CPROTEXT.COM online service allows you to protect texts published on the web from non authorized copies. This plugin integrates that service within the WordPress publication process in an unobtrusive way. It requires to have registered for a free account on the CPROTEXT.COM website

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

For security concerns, the e-mail address and the password associated with your CPROTEXT.COM account will never be stored in the WordPress database. This plugin will ask for your credentials when required.

FIRST STEPS

  1. Check that you have registered for a free account on CPROTEXT.COM.
  2. Click on the "CPROTEXT" tab in the WordPress "Settings" menu.
  3. Import your CPROTEXT.COM account settings.
  4. If not already available in the fonts list, upload the font that should be used to display your texts on this WordPress blog. (cf F.A.Q.)
  5. Select this font as the default font.
  6. Save these modifications.

After you wrote your text in the editor panel, you can ask for its protection by activating CPROTEXT before clicking the WordPress "Publish" button.

If you have any questions related to the CPROTEXT.COM online servive or to this plugin, please first check the F.A.Q. on the CPROTEXT.COM website.
